🗺 How to Bulk Upload to Google My Maps

Import sites-for-google-maps.csv in 5 steps:

1 Go to mymaps.google.com and click "+ Create a New Map"
2 In the left panel under "Untitled Layer", click "Import"
3 Upload sites-for-google-maps.csv from your visit-de-history folder
4 When prompted for the location column, select "GPS" first (or use "Address" as fallback)
5 When prompted for the title column, select "Name" — then click Finish. All 31 sites will appear as pins on the map.

💡 Tip: After import, you can color-code pins by County using the "Style by data column" option in Google My Maps.
